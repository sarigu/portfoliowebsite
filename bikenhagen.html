Be a bikenhagen

about the task

The project was about creating a website on biking in Copenhagen. We also had to plan, film and edit a video, that will later be shown on the site.

What I did

I joined the group after the XD prototype was made. I took part in the coding and filming.

The website

We wanted to create a microsite around the theme „be a bikenhagen“, which for us meant biking like the locals in Copenhagen and spending time outside in a healthy way. Therefore we put a lot of images on our website that refer to being outdoors.

Read more about the design in our documentation. Also have a look at the protoype if you'd like.

The video

We worked on a video that would translate a healthy, green lifestyle. When we developed the story, we wanted to adress the people who don’t bike a lot yet. So we came up with the story of a man who doesn’t exercise or eats well. After finding our page about biking, he tries it himself and falls in love with biking.

Read more about the idea in the treatment here and see our storyboard here.



The final version

We put everything together to create a microsite. See the website here.
