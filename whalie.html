Basic Animation

Whalie

about the task

The task was about creating a webpage that involves a character and 10 to 15 buttons. When the user clicks on one of the buttons, an animation should start.

What I did

The design

I drew the character in Illustrator.

The code

I chose 10 movements I wanted to animate in CSS.

  • Move: Make the character move from left to right continually.
  • Move to 30: Make the character move from the left side of the screen to 30% into the scene. Make the character slow down before it stops and stay at its final position.
  • Move from 30: Make the character move from 30% of the scene to the right outside. Make sure the it picks up speed.
  • One jump: Make the character jump and fall back nicely.
  • Jumping: Make the character jump up and down.
  • Fade: Make the character fade in and out.
  • Fall down: Make the character fall down.
  • Speak: Make the character look like it's speaking.
  • Shake: Make the character shake.
  • Move to 0: Move the character to the left side of the scene.
